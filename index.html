The Problem

Safety Binders
Don't Save Lives.

Small trade and manufacturing businesses face the same OSHA obligations as large employers — without the budget, staff, or tools to manage them. Until now.

01
Enterprise tools are out of reach

Platforms like VelocityEHS and Benchmark Gensuite cost $800–$5,000/month. Built for large corporations, priced out of reach for small businesses.

02
Independent consultants are expensive

Retaining a safety consultant typically runs $2,500–$3,500/month — consulting only, no software, no ongoing program management.

03
Doing nothing costs more

OSHA serious violation fines run up to $16,550 per violation. A single lost-time injury can drive workers' comp premiums up for years. The cost of inaction is not zero.
