The Problem

Your Crew Deserves
Better Than a Binder.

Small businesses face the same OSHA obligations as large employers — but the tools built to manage them were never designed for you. Until now.

01

Enterprise apps are overkill

Platforms like VelocityEHS and Benchmark Gensuite cost $800–$5,000/month. Complex to deploy, built for large corporations, and priced out of reach for small businesses.

02

Spreadsheets and binders don't work

Paper programs get ignored. Nobody pulls out the binder on a job site. A safety program your team can't access on their phone isn't really a safety program.

03

Doing nothing costs more

OSHA serious violation fines run up to $16,550 per violation. A single lost-time injury can drive workers' comp premiums up for years. The cost of inaction is not zero.

For less than $99/month, your entire team gets a mobile safety platform built by a CSP — inspections, training, hazard reporting, and SDS access, all in their pocket on day one.
