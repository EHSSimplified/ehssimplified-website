Who We Serve

Built for the Trades & Beyond

EHS Simplified is designed specifically for small businesses of 5–50 employees across high-risk industries. If your people work with their hands, we've got your back.

🏗️

Construction & Trades

Fall protection, toolbox talks, OSHA 10/30 alignment, subcontractor safety documentation, and site-specific hazard plans.

⚙️

Manufacturing

Lockout/tagout programs, machine guarding, hazard communication, and PPE assessments for general industry operations.

Hospitality & Food Service

Slip/trip hazard management, food handler safety, chemical handling, and ergonomics for kitchen and front-of-house staff.

Retail & Warehousing
