Common Questions

Before You Commit

What exactly is a "custom safety policy"?
A written, OSHA-aligned policy document tailored to your specific business — your industry, your hazards, your language. Not a generic template with your name pasted in. Examples include a Hazard Communication Program, a Lockout/Tagout policy, a Fall Protection Plan, or a Respiratory Protection Program.
What's a toolbox talk or training module?
A toolbox talk is a short, focused safety topic your supervisor can run in 10–15 minutes before a shift — written for your crew, in plain language. A training module is a more structured learning activity on a specific hazard or skill. Both are delivered as ready-to-use documents each month.
What does OSHA 300 log management mean?
We track, maintain, and file your OSHA injury and illness recordkeeping — the 300 Log, 300A Summary, and 301 Incident Reports — so you're always compliant with recordkeeping requirements and ready if OSHA ever comes knocking.
What happens if we have an incident on the Safety Starter plan?
You have access to add-on consulting at an hourly rate for any plan. On Safety Partner and above, incident investigation support is included — we help you document, investigate, and respond to workplace incidents as part of your subscription.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Yes. If your needs change during the year, reach out and we'll work with you. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades take effect at the next renewal period.
Is there a setup fee?
No setup fees on any plan. Done-for-you app configuration is included starting with Safety Starter — we load your SDS library, emergency contacts, and document portal as part of onboarding.
