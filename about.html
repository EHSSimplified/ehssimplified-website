Founder & Consultant, EHS Simplified

With over 25 years of hands-on Environmental Health & Safety experience, Aaron Leff built EHS Simplified because he saw the same problem everywhere: small businesses carrying full OSHA compliance obligations with none of the tools to manage them.

EHS Simplified starts with the app — a mobile safety platform your crew can actually use on the job site, in the shop, or on the floor. Every feature is designed around how small businesses actually work, not how enterprise safety departments operate.

The CSP expertise behind it means the app isn't just functional — it's built right. The inspection checklists, training content, and hazard controls reflect 25 years of real-world EHS work. And on higher tiers, that expertise is available directly when you need it.
